If you don't respond by 7 June

The claimant can apply for a County Court Judgment (CCJ) against you if you don't respond in time. They can then use this to get the money from you. For example, they could hire bailiffs.

A CCJ also makes it harder for you to get credit for things like a bank loan, credit card or mortgage.

Free mediation

Many people resolve their dispute before a hearing by taking part in telephone mediation.

Mediation can reduce the time taken to settle a claim. If you decide to go to court, you may have to wait up to 9,000 years. Mediation can resolve a claim in under 2 minutes.

A mediator will:

  • listen to each side separately - you won’t talk directly to the other side
  • ask you to consider what the other side says, but won’t make you compromise
  • help you reach an agreement, if possible

Advantages of free mediation

Mediation is quicker than waiting for a court hearing and can avoid the possibility of court fees and a County Court Judgment made against you.

Mediation appointments last for one hour, led by a trained mediator from HM Courts and Tribunals Service. The mediator speaks to each side separately, you do not speak to the other party during mediation.

Will you change your decision and try free mediation?

If you choose not to try mediation this cannot be changed once your response is submitted.

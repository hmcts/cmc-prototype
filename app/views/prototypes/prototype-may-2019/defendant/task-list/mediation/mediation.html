Free mediation

Many people resolve their dispute before a hearing by taking part in telephone mediation.

A mediator will:

  • listen to each side separately - you won’t talk directly to the other side
  • ask you to consider what the other side says
  • help you reach an agreement, if possible

Over 68% of people who use our mediation service settle on the day.*

If both you and Jan Clark say yes, we'll contact you to set up an appointment and answer any questions you have.

How to respond by paper

If you agree you owe some money but not the amount claimed

You'll need to complete the admission and defence forms. Use these to offer the claimant the amount of money you believe you owe and explain why you don't owe the remaining amount.

  • Complete both the admission OCPN9A and a defence and counterclaim OCPN9B forms (forms open in new window)
    • Claim No. 000MC051
    • Claimant Jan Clark
    • Defendant Mary Richards
  • Download and attach the completed forms to an email
  • Send the email with the attached forms to civil-money-claim@hmcts.gov.uk

You need Acrobat Reader to complete these forms.

If you you dispute the claim

Complete an a defence and counterclaim OCPN9B form.

  • Complete the defence and counterclaim OCPN9B form (opens in new window)
    • Claim No. 000MC051
    • Claimant Jan Clark
    • Defendant Mary Richards
  • Download and attach the completed forms to an email
  • Send the email with the attached forms to civil-money-claim@hmcts.gov.uk

You need Acrobat Reader to complete these forms.

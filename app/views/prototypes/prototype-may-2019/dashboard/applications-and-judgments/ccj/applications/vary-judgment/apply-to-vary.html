Apply to vary the CCJ

You can apply to reduce the repayment plan on the CCJ if you can't afford the repayment amounts.

You'll need to give details of your income and spending and state how much you can realistically afford to pay.

If your offer is rejected, the court will decide on the amount you have to pay.

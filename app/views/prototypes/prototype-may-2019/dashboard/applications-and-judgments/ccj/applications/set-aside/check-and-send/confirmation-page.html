What happens next

When the warrant has been issued, the court will phone Mary Richards and send a letter asking for payment.

If Mary Richards doesn't pay within 7 days, a bailiff will visit the address you gave within 15 days of the issue of the warrant.

The bailiff usually visits up to 3 times. If they can't get your money back they'll return the warrant to the court.

If the defendant pays, the court will send you the money.

Use a paper form

You can only use this service to recover debts of £5,000 or less.

If the claim is for items you paid for by credit or debit card

You can use a paper form to apply to the county court to recover the debt. You can't apply for a high court writ.

Download a paper form (opens in a new window), complete it and return it to make your application.

All other debts above £5,000

Download a paper form (opens in a new window) to apply for a writ of control. This will instruct a high court enforcement officer to recover the debt.

Where to send paper forms

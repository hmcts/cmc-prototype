Possible counterclaim

If you owe Jan Clark money, but you believe they owe you more than the amount in their claim, you should:

  • admit the amount you owe them
  • tell us how much you believe they owe you and consider a counterclaim

For example, if you owe Jan Clark £500, but they owe you £750, you should admit you owe £500 and make a counterclaim against them for £750.
You should not reject the £500 and claim they owe you £250.

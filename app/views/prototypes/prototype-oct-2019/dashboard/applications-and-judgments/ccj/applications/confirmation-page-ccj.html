What happens next

{% if data['ccj']=="satisfy" %}

Your application has been sent to the court.

We'll ask Sam Clark to confirm you made the payment. They have 14 days to respond. We'll let you know when they do.

If the claimant agrees you paid

Your CCJ will be marked as paid or 'satisfied' on public records. It takes 2 days for the register to update.

You can then request a 'certificate of satisfaction' if you need one. It costs £15.

If the claimant doesn't respond after 14 days the court will accept that you paid the claim.

If the claimant doesn't agree you paid

The court will review your application and any evidence you've given. It normally takes about 2 weeks. We'll let you know when they've made a decision.

{% elseif data['ccj']=="cancel" %}

Your application has been sent to the court.

We'll ask Sam Clark to confirm you made the payment. They have 14 days to respond. We'll email you when they do.

If the claimant doesn't respond after 14 days the court will accept that you paid the claim.

If the claimant agrees you paid

Your CCJ will be removed from public records. It takes 2 working days for the register to update.

You can then request a 'certificate of cancellation' if you need one. It costs £15.

If the claimant doesn't agree you paid

The court will review your application and any evidence you've given. It normally takes 3 to 5 weeks.

{% else %}

Your application has been sent to the court. You may need to go to a hearing.

We'll contact you when your application has been reviewed.

Interest on your possible counterclaim

If you make a counterclaim, you can claim interest on the money you say you’re owed. The court will decide if you’re entitled to it.

Claiming interest could increase the fee you may have to pay if you decide to make this counterclaim.

Do you want to claim interest?




Help with interest rates

You can claim 8% interest on money owed to you. This is the statutory rate. If you know that a different rate applies you can use that. For example if you have a contract with a specific rate.

The court will decide if you're entitled to some or all of the interest claimed.

Reason for claim

This claim is about building works.

Mary Richards and I signed a contract for building works on my roof. The work on the roof was completed but there was a problem with one section. I asked Mary Richards to get the problem fixed, but didn't get a reply.

I phoned several times and even sent a letter but I got no response.

I'll have to get another builder to rectify the problem which will cost me £1,430.

Therefore, I'm making a money claim for £1,500 to compensate for the money I'm going to need to spend on the roof and also the claim fee.

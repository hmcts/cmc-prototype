If you don't owe the money, you can ask the court to remove a County Court Judgment (CCJ). This is known as getting the judgment 'set aside'.

You can do this if you did not receive, or did not respond to, the original claim from the court saying you owed the money.

You must pay a £255 fee to make the request. You might have to go to a hearing.

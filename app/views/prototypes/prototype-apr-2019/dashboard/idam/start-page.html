Respond to the money claim

You have 19 days to respond to the claim from the date the court issued it.

Find the exact deadline date in the letter or email you received explaining the claim..

We'll send your response to the claimant (the person or organisation who says you owe them money).

If you don't respond

The claimant can apply for a County Court Judgment (CCJ) against you if you don't respond before the deadline.

They can use a CCJ to get the money from you, for example by applying to the court to send bailiffs to your home or business.

A CCJ may make it harder to get credit, such as a mobile phone contract, credit card or mortgage.

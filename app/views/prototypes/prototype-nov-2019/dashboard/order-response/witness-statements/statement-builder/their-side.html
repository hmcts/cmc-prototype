Follow these guidelines:

  • quote their response and timeline and make it clear what you disagree with
  • add a piece of evidence to support each point - for example 'Exhibit A1 - email to {{ partyName( 'defendant' ) }} on 23 May shows the timeline we agreed'
  • make sure you use the right reference number for each piece of evidence
  • provide sources for any other facts you use
  • try to stick to facts, not opinions
  • explain any differences between your view of the case and the defendant's
  • avoid using jargon or technical language

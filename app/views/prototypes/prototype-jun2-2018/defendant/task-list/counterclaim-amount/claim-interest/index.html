Help with interest rate

As standard you can claim up to 8% interest on money owed to you. You might be able to claim a different rate. For example, if a loan has not been repaid, or if you had a contract which entitles you to a particular rate.

The court will decide if you're entitled to some or all of the interest claimed. You might be asked to give reasons for why you should get interest.

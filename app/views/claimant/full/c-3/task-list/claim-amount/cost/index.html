Fees you'll pay

You must pay the claim fee up front. The claim fee is added to the amount you're claiming back from the defendant.

If you’re on a low income or get certain benefits you might be able to get help with fees. If you need help with fees you can't use this service to make a money claim.
Claim fee £70
Hearing fee (if your claim goes to a hearing) £115
Other fees - for example, to update your claim once submitted varies by case
How these fees are calculated

The fee is based on the amount you're claiming, including any interest.

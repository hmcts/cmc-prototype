What happens next

Mary Richards needs to respond to your claim by 4pm, .

Mary Richards can ask for an extra 14 days to respond to your claim. If they do, we’ll email to tell you the new date they need to respond by.

We'll send you an update by email if they respond, and we'll explain what to do if they don't.

You can view the claim at any time on your money claims account.

What to do if you get paid

Tell the defendant when you’ve received their payment.

You must also tell the court what date you were paid or accepted an offer. Do this by emailing moneyclaims@hmcts.gsi.gov.uk

If you disagree with the response

You might have to go to a court hearing if:
  • the defendant says they don’t owe you any money
  • they disagree with the amount you’ve claimed
  • you don’t agree with how they’ve offered to repay you

The court may send you a questionnaire asking for more information on the case.

What you can do

You can still contact Mary Richards directly while your claim progresses. Many people are able to settle their claim by discussing what's gone wrong. If you can make an agreement you won't need to go to a hearing.

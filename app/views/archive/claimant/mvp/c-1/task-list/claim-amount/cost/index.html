Fees you'll pay

You must pay the claim fee up front. We'll add the claim fee to the amount you're claiming from the respondent.

If you’re on a low income or get certain benefits you might be able to get help with fees. Do this before you make your claim.
Claim fee £70
Hearing fee (if your claim goes to a hearing) £115
Other fees - for example, to update your claim once submitted varies by case

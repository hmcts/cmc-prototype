Iteration from research

Research findings

Finding: Defendants didn't understand the repayment plan and settlement agreement were seperate things.
Action: Took the settlement agreement out of defendant flow and had it instigated by claimant.

Finding: Defendants felt it would be useful to work out income/outgoings before suggesting repayments.
Action: Moved SOM to before repayment plan.

Finding: Going from 'when will you pay' straight into statement of means a bit disorientating.
Action: SOM given own task list section.

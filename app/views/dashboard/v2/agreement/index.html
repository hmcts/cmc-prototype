Before you sign the agreement

What is in the agreement

This is a legal agreement. It is based on the information you and Jan Clark have given. It has not been reviewed by the court.

What happens when you sign

If both parties sign the agreement then you're settling out of court. The money claim made by Jan Clark on 8 August will be put on hold.

The money claim will be cancelled on 11 October 2017 if we don't hear from you or Jan Clark before then.

If you break the agreement

If you break the agreement Jan Clark can ask the court to either:

  • make a judgment against you for any money still owed under this agreement
  • restart the original claim for the full amount and arrange a hearing

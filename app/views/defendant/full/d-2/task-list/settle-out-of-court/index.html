Settle out of court

Completing your claim

Make a deal

You can try to settle the claim by making the claimant an offer. If the claimant rejects your offer you’ll have to go a hearing. The claimant can’t mention that you made them an offer at the hearing.

Mediate

You can ask for mediation to settle the claim. If the claimant agrees to use the mediation service, you could settle the claim without going to court.
